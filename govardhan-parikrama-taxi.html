Taxi for Govardhan Parikrama

Govardhan Parikrama is a sacred 21 km pilgrimage around Govardhan Hill in the Braj region. Devotees from all over India visit Govardhan to perform this holy Parikrama.

Braj Cab provides comfortable taxi services to reach Govardhan from Vrindavan, Mathura, and nearby cities. Our drivers are familiar with the Parikrama route and major temples along the way.

Important Places in Govardhan Parikrama

Danghati Temple

One of the most important temples in Govardhan visited by devotees during Parikrama.

Kusum Sarovar

Beautiful historic pond surrounded by sandstone architecture.

Radha Kund

Sacred kund considered extremely holy by Krishna devotees.

Mansi Ganga

Holy lake where devotees start the Govardhan Parikrama.
